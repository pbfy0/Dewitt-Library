Little Rock Nine Resources

 

Here's the official Little Rock Nine website, with some interesting links, such as the school newspaper from 1957-1958, a timeline of events, and a look at where the Little Rock Nine are today.

http://www.centralhigh57.org/index.html

 

This is the main page for African American Odyssey, a Library of Congress site. You'll find an interesting overview of the time period, and the Brown v. Board decision.

http://www.loc.gov/exhibits/odyssey/educate/marshall.html

 

These two links take you directly to images from African American Odyssey related to the Little Rock Nine:

http://memory.loc.gov/ammem/aaohtml/exhibit/aopart9.html#0911

http://memory.loc.gov/ammem/aaohtml/exhibit/aopart9.html#0918

 

Here is another Library of Congress site, "Brown v. Board at Fifty: the aftermath." Here you will find links, information, and images.

http://www.loc.gov/exhibits/brown/brown-aftermath.html

 

This is a page from Rethinking Schools Online, which offers ways to work with students when they read Warriors Don't Cry, and has some interesting reflection exercises and several photos.
