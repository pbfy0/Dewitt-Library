Garlic  

Allium sativum

 

Sowing instructions:  Plant cloves

When to sow: Fall (first frost)

Depth: 5 inches

Spacing:  6-12 inches apart

Direct or greenhouse:  direct

 

Harvest:  Harvest in the summer

Soil conditions

pH preference: 6.2-6.8

Drainage:  Prefers well drained soil

Moisture:  slightly dry

Fertility:  Plenty of organic matter, high fertility

 

Sunlight:  Full sun

 

Good Plant pairings:  beets and cabbage do well

Bad pairings:  beans, peas and potatoes

 

Special considerations: Garlic is unusual in that it grows over winter.  Plant garlic cloves 5 inches deep, then cover them with 5 inches of mulch over winter.  Remove mulch in the spring to allow for growth.  Keep enough mulch for weed control
