Things to consider when choosing crops:                                                                                                                           ------ In ground

1.  Which crops work well together?  (plant pairs, see plant pairs chart)

2.  Are you willing to plant crops that you won’t harvest until you’re in 8th grade or at all?                                    ------ In greenhouse

3.  Do your plants need a trellis or other special treatment?

4.  Are you willing to tend your seedlings in the greenhouse outside of class time?
